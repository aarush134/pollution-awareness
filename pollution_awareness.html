Pollution Awareness

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment, affecting air, water, and land.

Causes of Pollution

Pollution is caused by industrial waste, vehicle emissions, deforestation, and improper waste disposal.

Effects of Pollution

It leads to climate change, health issues, loss of biodiversity, and damage to ecosystems.

Consequences of Pollution

Pollution results in:

  • Severe health problems, including respiratory diseases, cancer, and heart disease.
  • Damage to ecosystems, leading to loss of species and biodiversity.
  • Global warming, affecting weather patterns, rising sea levels, and more extreme weather events.
  • Contamination of natural resources like water and soil, making them unsafe for consumption and agriculture.

How Can We Help?

Reduce waste, use eco-friendly products, plant trees, and support renewable energy.
